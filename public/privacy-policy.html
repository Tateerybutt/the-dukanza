Privacy Policy

At Tijva, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our website or place an order.

We collect only the information necessary to process orders, provide support, and improve your shopping experience.

How We Use It

Your information is used for order processing, delivery, customer support, and website improvements.

Data Security

We use secure cloud services and reasonable security measures to help protect your information.

Limited Sharing

Your information is shared only when necessary to complete your order or comply with legal obligations.

Cookies

Your Rights

Information We Collect

Your information is used to process orders, arrange deliveries, provide customer support, improve our services, prevent fraud, and communicate important order updates.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, remember your preferences, maintain your login session, and enhance your browsing experience.

You may request correction or deletion of your personal information by contacting our support team. Certain information may be retained where required for legal or operational purposes.

Policy Updates

Tijva may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will become effective immediately upon publication on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us through our Contact page or WhatsApp support.
